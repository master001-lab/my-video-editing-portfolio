Questions

Frequently Asked

What software do you use?

Adobe Premiere Pro for primary cuts, After Effects for motion graphics, DaVinci Resolve for cinematic colour grading, and AI tools for 3D style transfers and generative B-roll.

What's the turnaround time?

Short-form (Reels, TikToks, Shorts): 24–48 hours. Long-form YouTube: 4–7 business days. Complex commercial projects: scoped during onboarding.

Do you work with raw footage?

Yes. Send your raw footage via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or Frame.io. I handle everything from assembly cut to final export. No footage? I source and license stock video too.

How many revisions do I get?

Every project includes 2 structured revision rounds. Minor tweaks — colour notes, caption edits, trim adjustments — are unlimited within 30 days of delivery.
